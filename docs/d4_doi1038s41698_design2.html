Research Challenge

  • ⚡

    Complex Analysis

    Modern genetic tests identify hundreds of DNA variations, but determining clinical relevance requires extensive expertise

  • ⏱

    Time Intensive

    Expert pathologists spend hours analyzing literature and databases for each genetic variant

  • 👥

    Limited Scalability

    Shortage of specialized genomic medicine experts globally limits access to precision cancer care

  • 📊

    Consistency Issues

    Inter-observer variability among experts affects reproducibility of genetic variant interpretation

Institution: Taipei Veterans General Hospital & National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University
Funding: National Science and Technology Council, Taiwan

Key Findings

  • 🎯

    Performance Hierarchy

    GPT-4o demonstrated superior accuracy (73.2%) compared to Qwen 2.5 (57.3%) and Llama 3.1 (49.8%)

  • 🤝

    Consensus Accuracy

    When all three models agreed, classification accuracy reached 97%, approaching expert-level performance

  • 📚

    Knowledge Integration

    Retrieval-augmented generation significantly improved performance by incorporating external medical databases

  • ⚖️

    Conservative Approach

    Models exhibited cautious classification behavior, minimizing false positive treatment recommendations

Implications for Precision Medicine

This research demonstrates the potential for AI-assisted genetic variant interpretation to accelerate clinical decision-making, improve consistency in genomic medicine, and expand access to precision cancer care globally.
